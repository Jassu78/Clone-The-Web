Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Aura

Is Aura just another tablet?
No. Aura is purpose-built for kids - no open app stores, no social media, and no ads. It's focused purely on safe, educational experiences.
How is Aura's AI safe for children?
Aura's AI, Lulu, only engages with pre-approved, age-appropriate topics. Parents control what Lulu can discuss.
Will my child's data be collected or shared?
Never. Aura is designed with a privacy-first approach. No selling of personal data, no third-party tracking.
Can Aura be used without internet?
Yes. Core activities, games, and learning tools work offline, making it perfect for travel or low-connectivity areas.
How much control do parents really have?
Parents have full control over content access, screen time limits, AI topics, and browser permissions - all adjustable through simple settings.
Can kids accidentally access inappropriate content?
No. Aura features strict, curated browsing and AI filtering, ensuring a completely kid-safe digital environment.
Is there any advertising or in-app purchases?
None. Aura provides a clean, uninterrupted experience with no hidden purchases or commercial distractions.
Will my child get addicted to Aura like other screens?
Aura is designed to encourage healthy usage habits - with smart timers and psychologically healthy engagement patterns.
Is Aura durable enough for young kids?
Absolutely. It's lightweight, rugged, and built for active hands - with a tactile keyboard, joystick, and trackball to minimize drops.
